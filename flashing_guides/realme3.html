A Brief Guide for Newbies

Omkar

Want to get rid of stock ROM (realmeUI) & experience the AOSP ? Well it needs

- Unlocked bootloader of realme 3/ 3i which should be on realmeUI with latest fw(recommended)

- A Custom Recovery Installed,

So that, U can go for installing a Custom ROM


[UNLOCKING BOOTLOADER]

- Well it needs two devices, ofcourse ur realme 3/ 3i and a PC with platform tool & ADB & fastboot drivers installed properly (U can watch on yt how to install)

- U can also use second Android device with Bugjaeger app installed

- Now on realme 3/ 3i, turn on OEM Unlocking & USB Debugging from devloper settings

- Install this unlock tool, Submit in-depth test, wait to get it completed

- After Submission, Click on 'Start In-depth Test', OR connect to PC to give this adb cmd : adb reboot bootloader

- It will boot into fastboot now, connect to PC or Bugjaeger, Execute the following cmd:

- fastboot devices (to confirm connection)

- fastboot flashing unlock

- Follow what's written on fastboot screen & use Volume buttons to unlock

- fastboot reboot

After confirming unlock bl or Manually reboot and setup

And yeah bootloader unlocked successfully :P

[INSTALLING CUSTOM RECOVERY]

- Setup realme 3/ 3i, Again turn on USB Debugging

- Boot into fastboot by pressing volume down & power button when phone is powered off or by ADB cmd, adb reboot bootloader !

- Download & put a custom recovery in platform tools folder or in Bugjaeger installed device's internal storage (#recovery on our group)

- I recommend to flash pitch black 3.1.0 recovery as it's bug free & put recovery zip in sd card in the target device

Now connect to PC or Bugjaeger and execute these cmd

- fastboot flash recovery 'recovery.img'

- fastboot reboot recovery

- Once in recovery, flash recovery zip from sd card, reboot recovery. Delete vendor/ recovery-from-boot.p using recovery advanced/ file manager after mounting vendor to make recovery permanent on realmeUI

- Installing custom recovery is done


[FLASHING CUSTOM ROM]

- Put ROM zip, GApps, Para Patch etc in SD card in ur realme 3/ 3i & boot into recovery

- Once in recovery, Format Data only if internal storage is encrypted

- In advanced wipe, select Cache, Dalvik/ ART, Data, System, Wipe

- Never Wipe Vendor

- Locate ROM zip & flash

- In case of pitch black recovery uncheck 'Patch AVB 2.0 Verity & Disable

DM-Verity' options in flashing page

- After flashing ROM, Flash GApps( if ur ROM is Vanilla build)

- Flash Kernel, Magisk (Optional)

- Wipe Caches, Reboot System

- Flash Para Patch to avoid recovery loop if using recovery other than PitchBlack 3.1.0 Official

- Wait to get it booted, Setup

- Done!!!

[CHANGING ROM TO ROM]

- Take a Migrate/ Swift backup ( root needed ofc)

- Boot into recovery using advanced reboot

- Do the same as above xD

[NOTE]

- Do not try to flash any of these recovery or ROM on colorOS6 (May Brick) or any realme UI older fw like C09, C11, C13, C14 ( Won't get stability so Flash on latest fw like C19, C20, C21)

- Do not flash older Magisk like v21 etc
